# Task Chain: Brainstorm Solutions to a Problem

## Task Type
Brainstorm

## Purpose
HHS managers face persistent, wicked problems: staff retention, caseload management, community trust, inter-agency coordination, budget constraints. Brainstorming with AI is most valuable when it surfaces options you haven't considered — not just validates what you already know. This task chain generates a wide range of options and then helps you evaluate and prioritize them.

## Intake Question
"Describe the problem. What have you already tried or considered? I'll generate fresh angles."

## Full Prompt Chain

**Instruction:** Generate a wide range of potential solutions to this problem. I want ideas I haven't thought of yet — push past the obvious approaches.

**Context:** [User describes: the problem, constraints (budget, timeline, authority), what's been tried before, what's off limits]

**Constraints:** Generate at least 8 ideas across different categories (quick wins, systemic changes, cross-agency approaches, low-cost options, creative/unconventional ideas). Don't filter for feasibility in the first pass — generate broadly, then we'll evaluate.

**Output format:** Categorized list of ideas with a brief rationale for each. Then identify the top 3 most promising for our specific context.

## Power Follow-Up Options
- "Rank these by ease of implementation"
- "What's the cheapest option that might actually work?"
- "What would the most innovative county in California do here?"
- "Which of these could I start this week?"
- "What am I not thinking about?"

## Brainstorming Principles for HHS

The best brainstorms look across multiple domains: what have other counties done? What do best practices from adjacent fields suggest? What would a fresh perspective see that insiders miss?

HHS brainstorming is often constrained by what feels realistic given current budgets and authority. The most valuable ideas often come from temporarily suspending those constraints — generating what you'd do if resources weren't a limit, then working backward to find the affordable version.
